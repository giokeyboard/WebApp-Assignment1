Areas we focus on:

Area Details
Food collection Throughout the years we implemented a strategy to efficiently collect spare food from restaurant and supermarkets that otherwise would be wasted, causing serious harm to the environment as well as a great squander. Such recovered food are then distributed during 'street gatherings'.
Social housing iCare Foundation provides affordable housing to individuals or families without sound incomes or who experiment difficulties getting a house on the market. Rents are covered by donations and free-to-let lodgings.
Street gatherings Thanks to our supporters and volounteers around the world, iCare Foundation deploys more than a hundred fully-equipped caravans with washing machines, kitchens and showers. We provide hot meals and the chance to have a hot bath as long as making clothes clean and dry for people sleeping on the streets.
Psychological support Mental illness affect a person’s psychological, physical, emotional and spiritual wellbeing. All those aspects are seriously in danger among people who cannot afford a place to stay. Our psychologists work closely with local hospitals to support children, adults and elderly homeless to overcome everyday difficulties with the aim to let them reintegrate into society.
